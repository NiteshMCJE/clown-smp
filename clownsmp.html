SERVER RULES

1. Be respectful to all players and staff..

2. No griefing (destroying or altering others' builds without permission)..

3. No hacking or use of unfair mods.

4. Follow all staff instructions.

5. No stealing from other players.

6. No cheating or using hacked clients (X-ray, fly hacks, etc.).

7. No spamming or flooding the chat.

8. No advertising other servers or external websites.

9. Keep chat appropriate — no hate speech, racism, sexism, or inappropriate content.

10. No exploiting bugs or glitches — report them to staff.

11. Build responsibly — do not build inappropriate structures or near others without consent.
